Welcome to Chuy's Lawn Care & Maintenance LLC. Founded in 2019 by Chuy Pina, our company is dedicated to transforming outdoor spaces into stunning landscapes that fulfill our clients' dreams. Chuy's Lawn Care & Maintenance LLC. services the Northeast Indianapolis area as well as Fishers, Carmel, and Noblesville.


At our company, we understand that a well-groomed lawn is more than just grass; it's a source of pride and a place to relax and enjoy the outdoors. Our goal is to be your trusted partner in creating and maintaining a lush, vibrant lawn that enhances the beauty of your property.


We specialize in a range of essential lawn care services, including professional lawn mowing, meticulous mulch installation, thorough yard cleanups, and precise trimmings. Our experienced team uses state-of-the-art equipment and techniques to ensure that your lawn receives the care it deserves.


Contact us today to schedule an appointment, and let us take care of your lawn care needs. With our expertise and passion for creating exceptional outdoor spaces, we'll help you achieve a beautiful and healthy lawn that you can be proud of.
